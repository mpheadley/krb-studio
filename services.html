Our Services

Three Paths to
Finding Your Voice

Explore our three paths to musical confidence.

Singing Lessons

Personalized vocal coaching focused on confidence and the joy of music — not perfection. Whether you've never sung in front of anyone or you're returning to music after years away, Katie meets you exactly where you are.

  • Breath control and vocal technique
  • Building confidence through song
  • Genre exploration (pop, folk, classical, and more)
  • Performance preparation and stage presence
  • Custom song selection based on your interests

Musical Storytelling

Combine lyrics, rhythm, and narrative to find meaningful creative expression through song. This unique approach helps you connect your personal story with music in a way that feels authentic and empowering.

  • Lyric writing and interpretation
  • Connecting personal narrative to music
  • Rhythmic and melodic storytelling techniques
  • Creative expression through songcraft
  • Building emotional connection with your audience

Movement & Rhythm

Whole-body movement and dance that makes music a physical experience, not just academic. Using the body to understand rhythm and expression creates a deeper, more intuitive musical connection.

  • Rhythm training through body movement
  • Dance and expressive movement
  • Understanding music through physical awareness
  • Group and individual movement sessions
  • Integration with singing and storytelling
FAQ

Common Questions

I have no musical background. Can I still join?

Absolutely. We specialize in working with beginners and those who feel intimidated by traditional music education.

What exactly is "Movement through Music"?

It is a holistic approach that uses the body to understand rhythm and expression, making music a physical and emotional experience rather than just an academic one.

Are lessons online or in-person?

We offer in-person sessions at the Greenbriar Dear Entrepreneurial Center in Anniston, as well as online options for those who prefer the comfort of home.

Is there an age limit?

We teach all ages, from young children to adults. It is never too late — or too early — to start your musical journey.
