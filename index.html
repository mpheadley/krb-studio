The Problem

Music Shouldn't Feel Like
a Test You're Afraid to Fail

Most of us love music, but many feel like we aren't "musical enough" to participate in it. Whether it's the memory of a rigid classroom or the fear of hitting a wrong note, intimidation can keep you silent. At KRB Studio, we believe that everyone has a story to tell and a song to sing. You shouldn't have to be a prodigy to experience the emotional relief and confidence that music provides.

"Music doesn't get in. Music is already in. Music simply uncovers what is there, makes you feel emotions that you didn't necessarily know you had inside you, and runs around waking them all up. A rebirth of sorts." Matt Haig, How to Stop Time

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

I have no musical background. Can I still join?

Absolutely. We specialize in working with beginners and those who feel intimidated by traditional music education.

What exactly is "Movement through Music"?

It is a holistic approach that uses the body to understand rhythm and expression, making music a physical and emotional experience rather than just an academic one.

Are lessons online or in-person?

We offer in-person sessions at the Greenbriar Dear Entrepreneurial Center in Anniston, as well as online options for those who prefer the comfort of home.

Is there an age limit?

We teach all ages, from young children to adults. It is never too late — or too early — to start your musical journey.
