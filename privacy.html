Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 07, 2026

KRB Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website krbstudio.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

We may collect the following information when you voluntarily submit it through our contact form or by other means:

  • Message content and service interest

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment information, social security numbers, or government IDs through this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, which processes form submissions on our behalf. Formspree's privacy policy governs how they handle submitted data. We encourage you to review their policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Cookies & Tracking

This website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site, such as which pages are visited and how long users stay. This helps us improve the website experience. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to gather this information.

We only load tracking cookies after you give consent through the cookie banner shown when you first visit the site. If you decline, no tracking cookies are set and no data is collected. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Google's privacy policy governs how they process Analytics data: policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use Facebook Pixel, remarketing tags, or any other third-party tracking tools beyond Google Analytics.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Ask questions about this privacy policy

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy
