Meet Katie Burns — your guide to finding joy in music.

Singing · Storytelling · Movement

Meet Your Guide,
Katie Burns

With years of experience teaching woodwinds, theory, and movement across the globe, Katie Burns brings a unique, multi-disciplinary approach to Anniston.

As a staff member of CAST Kidz and CAPA, she is deeply embedded in the local arts community and understands the transformative power of performance.

Based in the professional environment of the Greenbriar Dear Entrepreneurial Center, Katie provides a sanctuary for students of all ages who are ready to discover their musical interests and passions.

Why KRB Studio

Music Shouldn't Feel Like a Test

Most of us love music, but many feel like we aren't "musical enough" to participate in it. Whether it's the memory of a rigid classroom or the fear of hitting a wrong note, intimidation can keep you silent.

At KRB Studio, we believe that everyone has a story to tell and a song to sing. You shouldn't have to be a prodigy to experience the emotional relief and confidence that music provides.
